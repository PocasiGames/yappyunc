2. The app (YappyUnc)

  • No servers run by the developer, no user accounts, no sign-up.
  • Your best score and audio settings are stored locally on your device. This locally saved data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app. If you are signed in to Game Center, the score of a finished run is additionally submitted to Apple's Game Center leaderboard (see section 3).
  • No ads, no analytics or tracking SDKs, no in-app purchases.

3. Apple Game Center (leaderboard)

If you are signed in to Game Center, your score and Game Center nickname are handled by Apple's Game Center service so your ranking can be shown on the leaderboard. This is done by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer does not receive your name, Apple ID, or email. Signing in to Game Center is optional — without it, no score leaves your device.

Legal basis: legitimate interest in offering a leaderboard (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

4. This website (hosting via GitHub Pages)

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc., a Microsoft company, USA). When you open the site, GitHub as the host processes technically necessary access data (e.g. IP address, date and time, the page requested, browser and operating system, referrer URL) in server log files in order to deliver the site and ensure its security and stability.

Legal basis: legitimate interest in the secure, reliable provision of the site (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

The site sets no tracking cookies. To remember your chosen language, a small, technically necessary preference may be stored locally in your browser (localStorage); it serves only that function and contains no personal data.

5. Contact by email

If you email us, we process your email address and your message in order to answer your request. Legal basis: legitimate interest and/or (pre-)contractual communication (Art. 6(1)(f) and (b) GDPR). We delete the correspondence once it is no longer needed and no retention obligations apply.

6. International data transfers

GitHub/Microsoft may process data in the USA. This transfer is safeguarded by the EU–US Data Privacy Framework (adequacy decision) and, in addition, by Standard Contractual Clauses. Apple likewise processes Game Center data partly internationally on a corresponding legal basis.

7. Storage & deletion

  • App data: stored locally until you reset it or delete the app.
  • Server logs: retained only briefly by the host.
  • Email correspondence: kept until your request is resolved, unless legal obligations require longer retention.

8. What the app does NOT do

  • No in-app purchases — the game is completely free.
  • No ads.
  • No analytics, tracking SDKs, or tracking cookies.
  • No profiling.
  • No account or sign-up.
  • No selling or sharing of your data with third parties for advertising.

9. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access (Art. 15), rectification (16), erasure (17), restriction (18), data portability (20) and objection (21), and the right to withdraw any consent you have given.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR), in particular in the member state of your residence, place of work, or the place of the alleged infringement.

For any request, contact pocasigames@icloud.com.

10. Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children.

11. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
