Privacy Policy

YappyUnc · Last updated 27 June 2026

YappyUnc is built to respect your privacy. The short version: the app does not collect, store, or share any personal data on its own servers — because it has none.

Data the app stores

  • Your best score and audio settings are saved locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

Apple Game Center (leaderboard)

If you use the leaderboard, your score and Game Center nickname are handled by Apple's Game Center service so your ranking can be shown. This is done by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer does not receive your name, Apple ID, or email through this.

What the app does NOT do

  • No in-app purchases — the game is completely free.
  • No ads.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No account or sign-up.
  • No data sold or shared with third parties.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children.

Your rights (GDPR / DSGVO)

Since the developer holds no personal data about you, there is nothing to request, correct, or delete on the developer's side. For data held by Apple (Game Center), please refer to Apple. Questions? Contact CONTACT@EXAMPLE.COM.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
